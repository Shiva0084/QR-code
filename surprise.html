Month 1 – “The Spark”

January 29 – February 28, 2025

My Dearest Star,

They say the best stories begin when you least expect them — and ours started in the middle of a small crowd, on a regular day that turned out to be anything but ordinary.

Republic Day 2025 — I didn’t know you, you didn’t know me. And yet, something about that day still lingers in my heart.

Then came 29th January, the day that now holds a special place in my soul. It was just a brief virtual glimpse of you, but something about you stayed with me. Something warm. Something unforgettable. I didn’t realize it then, but that was the beginning — the first real moment where the spark turned into a flame.

We met for the first time on 3rd February, outside the IT lab, and even though it was short, it was enough. That memory is etched in my heart. I still remember the butterflies, the shy excitement, and how my world felt just a little brighter after.

Our chats grew deeper. Our connection felt easy, natural — as if our souls already knew each other. I found myself waiting to hear from you, to talk to you, to share even the tiniest bits of my day with you. And you listened — really listened — like no one ever had before.

The more I got to know you, the more I realized something special was unfolding. Even when 28th February didn’t go as planned — even though I wanted to ask you something that day and everything flopped — I saw a deeper beauty in how you were just you. Tired, human, real — and still, the person I wanted to be next to.

That first month? It changed everything. It lit the spark that still burns in me every single day.

Love,
Your Pikachu 💛

Month 2 – “Getting Closer”

March 1 – March 31, 2025

My Sweetu,

If the first month was the spark, then this was when the fire truly caught on.

6th March 2025 — I’ll never forget that day. I was nervous, clumsy, hopeful, and hopelessly in love. I still laugh thinking about my words:
"Would you be my Ash? 😳"
Corny? Maybe. Sincere? Absolutely.

And you said yes.
That moment — that yes — became a part of me forever.

After that, everything felt warmer, more magical. Although we were far away after that but still. We weren’t just two people talking anymore — we were us. Sharing little things, comforting each other, talking for hours about life, dreams, random thoughts. My heart started learning a new rhythm — and it beat with your name.

You made me feel seen. Valued. Loved. Not for anything big or dramatic, but just for being who I was. And I began falling — deeper than I ever imagined possible.

I remember the joy of being around you. The way time would bend when we were together. Every hug, every smile, every silly nickname we gave each other — they became pieces of this mosaic we were slowly building.

We were getting closer, not just in time spent but in soul. I started to understand your silences, your laughter, your tired eyes after long days. And somewhere in between all that, I saw the girl I wanted to grow with.

I was no longer just me. I was your Deepu. Your Pikachu. Your person. And that feeling — it was everything.

Forever close,
Your Deepu 💛

Month 3 – “Falling Deeper”

April 1 – April 30, 2025

My Gunn,

This month was a beautiful chaos — end sems, stress, revision marathons — and yet, somehow, amidst the madness, we created some of the most unforgettable memories of our relationship.

We balanced the pressure of exams with warmth and comfort. Cheering each other on, motivating when one of us felt tired, reminding each other to rest, to eat, to breathe. It was exhausting — but we did it, together. And in doing so, we grew even closer.

But you know what moment changed everything for me?

Our first kiss.

It wasn’t perfect in the cinematic sense — but it was ours. Honest. Raw. Magical. That moment still plays in my mind like my favorite movie scene — where everything goes quiet and the world disappears, leaving just us.

That kiss wasn't just physical — it was a promise. A silent confession. A bridge between two hearts that were already falling, but now were freefalling — completely and irreversibly.

This month, I understood the depth of what we had. I realized I wasn’t just falling in love — I was in love. Fully. Fiercely.

And even in the madness of exams, your voice, your laugh, your presence — kept me sane. You became my calm and my spark, all at once.

Here’s to kisses, to chaos, to falling deeper —
And most importantly, to you.

With all my heart,
Your forever Starboy 💛

Month 4 – “Growing Together”

May 1 – May 31, 2025

My Swasu,

This month wasn’t easy. Not for me — and I know, maybe not for you either.

Being away from you, truly away, felt like a storm brewing in my chest every day. I tried to stay strong. I tried to keep my mind busy — music, classes, studies, distractions of all kinds. But no matter what I did, one thing stayed constant:

I missed you. Every single day.
Not a moment passed without your name echoing somewhere in my heart.

And yet, even through the distance, through all the noise and silence, there was something beautiful we never let go of — those little rituals, the lifelines.
Not one day went without a “Good morning” or a “Good night.”
And that, in itself, is love — steady, soft, and resilient.

There were storms in my head — doubts, fears, moments when I questioned everything.
“What if I lose her?”
That thought haunts me sometimes. And I wish I could erase it, but maybe it’s just the cost of loving someone so much that the idea of losing them feels unbearable.

But despite all the questions and chaos, one truth remained:
I don’t want anyone else. I want you. Just you.

This month taught me how deeply I need your presence — not just physically, but emotionally, spiritually. I realized how much I rely on your love, your voice, your support. And how, even from miles away, you still manage to make my heart feel full.

I know we’ve had our share of ups and downs. I know we’re still learning how to be strong from afar. But if growing together means facing storms like these and still choosing each other — again and again — then I’ll walk through every season with you.

Because no matter how hard it gets, I’ll always find my way back to you.

You are my comfort. My fear. My peace. My storm.
My sweetest pain and my warmest home.

Let’s keep growing — together. Even in distance.
Even in doubt.

With all the love I carry,
Your Deepu 💛

Month 5 – “Still With You”

June 1 – June 30, 2025

My Star,

As we step into our fifth month, I’m amazed by how far we’ve come — and how much more I’ve fallen for you, every single day. It’s crazy to think that soon we’ll be celebrating six months together. Half a year. Half a beautiful chapter of something I never imagined I’d be lucky enough to have.

You’re special — you know that, right?
And I try, in my own ways, to remind you of that — through words, through tiny acts, through the time I give you from my heart. But sometimes, even amidst all this love, there are things I find hard to admit. Things like… insecurity.

I know I’ve been a little off lately. I asked you something on 3rd May, and I know it wasn’t the smartest way to express myself. Maybe I was scared. Maybe I was foolish. Or maybe I just love you so much that the fear of losing you sometimes grows too loud in my head.

I’m trying, I really am. I try to stay calm, to trust, to remind myself of everything we’ve built. But sometimes, my heart runs ahead of me — dreaming of you, loving you deeply — and then panicking at the thought of not being enough.

You are in my sweetest dreams — and also in my worst nightmares. That’s how much space you hold in me.

I’ll keep trying to be better — for you, for us. And if there are moments when I stumble, when my fear outweighs my reason, I just ask you one thing:
Handle me with care.

Because every boy, I think, is a little insecure for his girl — especially when she’s as rare and radiant as you.
You are not just my girl.
You’re my Star — made of stardust, my little universe, my miracle.

With you always,
Your Pikachu 💛
